Corn Dog

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Oven

    Preheat oven 180c with an oven tray and greaseproof paper

  • Step 1

    Whisk 120g wholewheat white flour, 155g yell cornmeal, 1tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp ground mustard/in a bowl.

  • Step 2

    Cut in 85g butter until breadcrumbs (similar to scones), then stir in 120ml milk and 85g honey.

  • Step 3

    Roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness, skewer hot dogs and wrap dough around, cutting to shape.

  • Step 4

    Place in oven at 180c for 15-20 mins or until browning.
