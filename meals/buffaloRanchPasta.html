Buffalo Ranch Pasta

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Fry the 3 minced garlic cloves in a large pot for a couple mins, then toast 1 tbsp nutritional yeast, 2 tsp dried parsley, 1 tsp dried dill, 1/2 tsp onion powder.

  • Step 2

    Add 225g dry pasta, 770ml water, 60ml buffalo sauce to the pot, stir and boil

  • Step 3

    Cook the pasta, likely be the longer end of the time on the packet.

  • Step 4

    Mix 2 tsp cornstarch with some cold water, and add to the pasta as needed to thicken, then stir in 2 tbsp cream cheese, taste (more salt, hot sauce etc) and serve.
