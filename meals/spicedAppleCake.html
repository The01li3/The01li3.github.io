Spiced Apple Cake

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat oven to 180, and grease an 8-inch square pan, using a sling. In a medium bowl whisk 280g plain flour, 1 tsp bicarb, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1/2 tsp ground allspice, 1/2 tsp nutmeg, 1/4 tsp ground cloves and 1/2 tsp salt .

  • Step 2

    In a large bowl beat 112g butter until light, then mix in 200g sugar. Add in the 3 large eggs one at a time, beating after each.

  • Step 3

    Add in 1/2 tsp vanilla and apple sauce, with a final beat until smooth.

  • Step 4

    Mix in half of the dry, then 60g buttermilk, then the rest of the dry, the 60g chopped walnuts can then go in, don't over-mix.

  • Step 5

    Pour into the 8 inch sq tin and bake for 25-35 mins, let cool for 15 mins before removing from the tin.
