Sticky Lemon Tofu

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Fry 4 minced garlic cloves and 3cm grated ginger for a min

  • Step 2

    Then add 60ml water, 1/4 stock cube, 60ml lemon juice, 1 lemon zest, 1 tbsp soy sauce and 50g sugar and stir

  • Step 3

    Mix 2 tsp cornstarch with some water and add, letting it simmer until thick.
