Black Bean and Seitan Stir Fry

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Sauce

    For the sauce, mix in half of the can of black beans, 30g dark brown sugar, 3 peeled garlic cloves, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp Chinese 5 spice, 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp peanut butter, 1 red chilli and 50ml water. Gently heat in saucepan for 5 mins.

  • Seitan

    Treat similar to tofu. Cut, dry, marinate, toss in cornflour and fry.

  • Final

    Fry any toppings in a frying pan, add in seitan, stir in sauce, serve.
