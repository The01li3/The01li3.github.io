Garlic and Ginger Curry

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Toast 2 tsp fenugreek seeds for a min, then add 1 diced onion and fry for 3 mins until soft. Add 4 minced garlic cloves, 3cm ginger and 1 finely chopped green chilli cook for another min.

  • Step 2

    Add 1 1/2 tsp garam masala, 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp chilli powder and a little black pepper, cook until fragrant.

  • Step 3

    Add 2 tbsp tomato puree, 1 can chopped tomato, 350ml of stock and 1 tbsp mango chutney, cook for 15-20 mins until thickened. If adding fish, add according to instructions, normally 10-15 mins from frozen.
