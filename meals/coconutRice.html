Coconut Rice

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Rinse 400g jasmine ricein cold water until runs clear, and add to pot.

  • Step 2

    Add 1 can of coconut milk, 340ml water, 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp sugar stir and bring to the boil.

  • Step 3

    When boiling, cover on low heat for 20 mins, then uncovered for 5.

  • Step 4

    Take off heat, fluff and serve.
