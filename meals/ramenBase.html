Ramen Base

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Add 2 tsp minced ginger and 1 tbsp minced garlic to a saucepan with oil and fry.

  • Step 2

    Add 3 tbsp soy sauce and 2 tbsp mirin and cook for a further min before adding in the stock and let simmer for 5 mins.

  • Eggs

    Eggs take 7-8 mins depending on size to soft boil, add to cold water after to halt cooking.

  • Dried mushrooms

    Hydrate mushrooms in hot water for 10 mins, use this water in the stock.

  • Additional toppings

    Gyoza: cook as per pack normally 4 mins.
    Noodles: cook as per pack normally 2-3 mins.
    Bok Choi: 2 mins for the white, 1-2 mins for the greens.
    > Tofu: a few mins just to heat up.
    Beansprouts: a few mins just to heat up.
    Chicken: roast chicken can just be added on top.
