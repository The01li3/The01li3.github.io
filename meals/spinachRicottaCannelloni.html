Spinach and Ricotta Cannelloni

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat oven to 180, Cook down/defrost the 300g spinach with a little salt and pepper, drain.

  • Step 2

    Mix together the 180g ricotta, 15g parmesan and cooked drained spinach, seasoning to taste

  • Step 3

    Mix the 500g passata with the 1/2 tsp garlic granules and 1/2 tsp dried Italian herbs, then pour half of it over the an oven proof dish.

  • Step 4

    Fill a piping bag with the spinach mixture, and fill 8 cannelloni tubes with the mixture, do not overfill as you'll fun our of mixture. Place tubes on the sauce in the dish, then cover in remaining sauce.

  • Step 5

    Cover in the 70g mozzarella, and 20g cheddar (paprika if needed) then place in oven at 180 for 35 mins.
