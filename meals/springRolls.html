Spring Rolls

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    After prepping the veg into matchsticks, fry 6 spring onions, 2 garlic cloves, 2cm ginger and 1 small red chilli for 1 min. Then add the carrot and 1/2 red pepper.

  • Step 2

    Finally add 70g beansprouts, 1 tbsp dark soy sauce and a pinch of chinese 5 spice. Transfer to a plate after a min to cool.

  • Step 3

    Place 3 of the filo sheets on top of each other 8 times and add 1/8th of the filling to each, brush beaten egg around all sides. Fold the bottom up, sides in, then roll finishing seem side down.

  • Step 4

    Airfry at 170 for 8 mins and check. Turn if needed
