Cod In Romesco Sauce

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Oven

    Preheat fan oven/ air fryer to 180c.

  • Sauce Step 1

    Add 5 roughly chopped tomatoes, 1 roughly chopped red onion, 1 roughly chopped red bell pepper, 1 red chilli and 5 garlic cloves to a baking tray drizzle in olive oil and bake for 15 mins at 180.

  • Sauce Step 2

    Add the bread, then bake for another 10 mins.

  • Potatoes

    Bring a pan of salted water to the boil and add 500g of new potatoes (halved ish) boiling for 15 mins. Drain and set aside when done.

  • Fish

    Add a little oil to a frying pan and fry 2 code fillets skin side down for 4 mins. Flip and add to oven for 5 (or flip and keep frying until done).

  • Sauce Step 3

    When vegetables are done remove, and add to a processor, add in 2 tbsp red wine vinegar and a pinch of salt, then blend until smooth.

  • Assembly

    Sauce can go over potatoes and fish, top with basil leaves if you have any, side of veg/salad/broccoli etc.
