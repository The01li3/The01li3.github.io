Lemon Stir Fry

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    In a jug mix together 2 tsp honey, 2 juiced lemons, 150ml chicken stock and 2 tbsp soy sauce.

  • Step 2

    Toss 4 chunked chicken breasts> in cornflour, or coating equivalent, and fry in a pan until crisp

  • Step 3

    Add 2 finely sliced carrots and 1 sliced red pepper fry for 1 min, then pour in 150ml chicken stock and bring to the boil.

  • Step 4

    Add 140g sugar snap peas and simmer for 5 mins till chicken is cooked.
