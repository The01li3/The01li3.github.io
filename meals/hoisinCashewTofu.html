Hoisin Cashew Tofu

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Prep tofu as needed to marinate it and fry it etc.

  • Step 2

    Whisk 60ml hoisin sauce, 60ml water, 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp soy sauce and 2 tsp cornstarch in a bowl

  • Step 3

    Fry the white parts of the 8 spring onions and 4 minced garlic cloves for 1 min, then add in the tofu for a further 3. Add the hoisin sauce and simmer for 5 mins.

  • Step 4

    Reduce the heat, add in 107g cashews and the greens of the 8 spring onions and serve.
