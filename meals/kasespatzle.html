Kasespatzle

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Caramelise the 3 sliced large onions by frying with the 6 tablespoons butter covered on low for 30 ish mins

  • Step 2

    Layer 1/3 of the cooked Spaetzle (can add a little butter to the pasta if needed) into a casserole dish, followed by 1/3 of the cheese and 1/3 caramelised onions, sprinkling salt between each layer

  • Step 3

    Bake for 10 mins at 180 until the cheese has melted, serve immediately
