Banana Cake

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    125g Butter, 150g caster sugar, 2 eggs and 1 tsp vanilla wizz until smooth and creamy.

  • Step 2

    Add 3 very ripe bananas, 85g chocolate chips, 90g SF flour, 90g plain flour and 1 tsp bicarb and blend until flour is mixed in.

  • Step 3

    Pour into a 20cm ring tin, can use other tins, but this stops a soggy middle, even loaf tins seem too thick.

  • Step 4

    Cook on the middle shelf at 170c (fan) for 30-35 mins, test with skewer.
