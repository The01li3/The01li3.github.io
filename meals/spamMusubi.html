Spam Musubi

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Stir 100g sugar, 60ml light soy and 60ml oyster sauce in a bowl until sugar is dissolved.

  • Step 2

    Cut the tin of Spam into 10 slices, or whatever thickness you need and marinate in marinade, the longer the better overnight if possible.

  • Step 3

    Over medium/high heat, cook the spam, 2 mins each side.

  • Step 4

    Cut nori sheets to needed thickness (1/2 or 1/3rds) and fry for a few seconds to crispen.

  • Step 5

    Press rice in a spam sized rectangle about 1 inch thick, place spam on top, and roll nori around it.
