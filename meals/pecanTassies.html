Pecan Tassies

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Tart shells, beat 115g butter and 100g soft cheese in a bowl until smooth, add 140g plain flour and mix until a soft dough.

  • Step 2

    Shape into 1 inch balls, place balls into un-greased mini muffin pan, and dib with mini tart shaper, flouring the shaper as needed.

  • Step 3

    Melt 25g butter in a small bowl, stir in 175g light brown sugar, 1 egg and 1tsp vanilla essence. Add 115g chopped pecans stir and fill casings.

  • Step 4

    Bake for 20-25 mins at 170c until light golden brown, cool in pan.
