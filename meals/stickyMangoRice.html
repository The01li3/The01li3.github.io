Sticky Mango Rice

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Cover 200g sticky rice with water and leave to rest for an hour, or overnight.

  • Step 2

    Drain and steam for 20 mins, sieve in a lidded saucepan works fine.

  • Step 3

    In a small saucepan add half a can of coconut milk, 3/4 of the sugar (75g) and a large pinch of salt. When the rice is cooked, add this and the rice to a large bowl, mix, cover with plastic and leave for 20 mins.

  • Step 4

    Add remaining half a can of coconut milk with 2 tsp cornstarch to the saucepan and heat for 3 mins. whisk in the remaining 25g sugar and a large pinch of salt.

  • Step 5

    Add rice, mango and sauce to a plate, serve.
