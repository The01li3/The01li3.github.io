American Pancakes

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Mix together 135g plain flour, 1 tsp baking powder, 1⁄2 tsp salt, 2 tbsp caster sugar

  • Step 2

    Whisk 1 Large Egg into 130ml milk with 2 tbsp olive oil. Pour into the flour mixture and beat until smooth. Leave for a few minutes.

  • Step 3

    Let the mixture sit while you heat up the cast iron pan on med heat, just right of south on the larger burner.

  • Step 4

    Over medium heat, melt knob of butter and add a ladle of batter. Wait until the top of the pancake begins to bubble, turn it over and cook the other side. The pancake will rise to about 1cm thick. Repeat!
