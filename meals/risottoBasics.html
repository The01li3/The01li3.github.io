Risotto Basics

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Prep the stock. 1l of stock-ish per 175g of risotto (about 2 people), it'll need to be warm when used, so make fresh.

  • Step 2

    Fry 1 diced onion to soften, 10 mins is normally ok, garlic needs about 1 min. Then fry the rice for 2 mins to help avoid it becoming mushy.

  • Step 3

    Add in the wine/black garlic/cherry tomatoes etc. (if using) then the stock a ladle at a time, making sure it absorbs between each ladle. (25-30 mins)

  • Step 4

    After the rice is cooked (20-25mins), take off the heat and stir through 20g parmesan and butter/butternut squash mash/mascarpone/sage etc. (if using) top with fried sage/butter nut squash chunks/remaining parmesan and serve.
