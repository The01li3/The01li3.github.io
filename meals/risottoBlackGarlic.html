Black Garlic Risotto

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Prep the stock. 1l of stock-ish per 175g of risotto (about 2 people), it'll need to be warm when used, so make fresh.

  • Step 2

    Fry 180 diced spring onion to soften, 10 mins is normally ok, garlic needs about 1 min. Then fry the rice for 2 mins to help avoid it becoming mushy.

  • Step 3

    Add in the black garlic (it'll dissolve in the stock with mixing), then the stock a ladle at a time, making sure it absorbs between each ladle. (25-30 mins)

  • Step 4

    After the rice is cooked (20-25mins), take off the heat and stir through 20g parmesan and 30g very cold butter top with remaining parmesan and serve.
