Low Calorie Pizza

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat oven to 200c, preheat the baking tray/pizza stone

  • Step 2

    In a mixing bowl add of self raising flour, of Greek yoghurt until combined, then add 1/2 tsp salt and 2 tsp mixed herbs and shape into a smooth ball.

  • Step 3

    Move dough to a lightly floured grease proof paper, place another on top, and roll a rolling pin over to flatten, 1/4 inch thickness-ish.

  • Step 4

    Precook the dough for 10 mins at 200c, flipping at 7 mins, while the topping are prepped.

  • Step 5

    Take out the pizza, add toppings to the most cooked side, and put back in to cook the toppings (5-6 mins).
