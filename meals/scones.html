Scones

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Oven and Dish

    Preheat to 200c fan and lightly grease 2 baking trays

  • Step 1

    Add 450g self raising flour, 2 tsp baking powder and 50g caster sugar in a bowl. Add 100g cubed soft butter and rub in with fingertips until fine breadcrumbs.

  • Step 2

    Mix 2 large eggs to a measuring bowl and top up to 300ml with milk. Using a fork or round tipped knife stir this mixture into the flour until a sticky dough.

  • Step 3

    Roll out dough onto a floured work surface about 2cm thick. Then use a fluted 5cm cutter pushing down and not twisting. Place on oven tray and repeat until no dough is left.

  • Step 4

    Brush the top with milk/left over mixture and bake for 12-15 mins at 200c until risen and golden.
