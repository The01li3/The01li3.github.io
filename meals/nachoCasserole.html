Nacho Casserole

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Combine 400g black beans, 175g sweetcorn, 120g sliced olives.

  • Step 2

    In the casserole dish, half the enchilada sauce, half the nachos, half the bean mixture, half of the grated cheese.

  • Step 3

    Then add the remaining nachos, bean mixture, enchilada sauce, cheese, and any additional toppings.

  • Step 4

    Bake for 20 mins at 170, until heated through and bubbling.
