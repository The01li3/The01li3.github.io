Tikka Masala Salmon

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    mix 50g fat free greek yoghurt, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp garam masala, 1/2 tsp ground coriander, 1/2 tsp paprika into a bowl. Chop salmon into chunks and add to the marinade. Leave for at least 10 mins

  • Step 2

    Spray a frying pan with low calorie spray and add in the salmon chunks, cook for 5 mins, turning so all sides are lightly browned. place on plate to one side.

  • Step 3

    Spray the same pan, then add 1 diced onion, 1 diced yellow pepper cook for 4 mins. Add 100g grated courgette, 2 minced garlic cloves and 5cm grated ginger for 2 mins.

  • Step 4

    Add 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp ground coriander, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garam masala to coat the veg, then add 1 can of chopped tomato, 100g passata, 1 tbsp tomato puree and 50ml vegetable stock and stir.

  • Step 5

    Simmer for 5 mins, add the salmon back, then simmer for 5 more. Serve.
