Pumpkin and Spinach Lasagne

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Oven

    Preheat to 180c, lasagne oven dish ready.

  • Step 1

    Cook the starch, 10 mins boiling or longer frying until soft, mashing when ready.

  • Step 2

    Blanch 3 frozen cubes of spinach in water/defrost. Then mix cooked starch, spinach and 250g low fat cottage cheese in a bowl.

  • Step 3

    Add 1 tbsp dried oregano and 1 tbsp dried basil to 400ml of passata.

  • Step 4

    In an oven dish place half of the mixture, 3.5 lasagne sheets, half the sauce. Then the rest of the mixture, 3.5 lasagne sheets, sauce, then top with 60g light cheddar and 60g light mozzarella

  • Step 5

    Bake at 180c for 30 mins, or until browning on top.
