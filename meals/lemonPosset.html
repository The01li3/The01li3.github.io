Lemon Posset

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Put 600ml double cream into a large saucepan with 200g caster sugar and heat gently stirring until melted. Then simmer for 1 min.

  • Step 2

    turn off heat and stir in 3 lemon zest and 75ml of lemon juice.

  • Step 3

    Divide between bowls, leave to cool, then in the fridge for at least 3 hours, or overnight.
