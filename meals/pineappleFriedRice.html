Pineapple Fried Rice

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Whisk 2 eggs and add to a oiled frying pan getting it up the sides to make a thin omelette. Once cooked remove, roll and cut into ribbons.

  • Step 2

    Add 2 crushed garlic cloves and 4 chopped spring onions to the pan to stir fry.

  • Step 3

    Add 300g cooked rice and 100g frozen peas. Wait a few mins then add in 1 tsp sesame oil and 1 tbsp light soy sauce

  • Step 4

    After cooking, add in 200g pineapple chunks and the egg ribbons until warmed through then serve.
