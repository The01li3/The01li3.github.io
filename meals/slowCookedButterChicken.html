Slow Cooked Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Cut 900g of chicken into bite sized pieces, and dice 1/2 an onion. Fry onion lightly for 5 mins.

  • Step 2

    Add 3 minced garlic cloves, 1 tbsp red curry paste, 2 tsp curry powder, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp ground ginger, 1 can of coconut milk, small can tomato paste and 2 tbsp butter.

  • Step 3

    Slow cook on low for 6-8 hours, or high for 3-4. Stir in 60g double cream while you make any rice.
