Tempeh Tacos

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Cut the 227g block tempeh into thick strips, and add to a frying pan with 237ml stock. Boil, then simmer for 5 mins.

  • Frying

    Reduce the heat and add the required sauce, flipping to get an even coat and cook.

  • Grill

    Grill with a required sauce coating brushing and turning frequently.
