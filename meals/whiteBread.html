White Bread

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Add the 7g of instant yeast into 100ml of warm water and a punch of sugar, adding to a proving drawer for 20-30 mins to test the yeast.

  • Step 2

    Add 450g of brea or plain white flour, 1 tsp of salt and 10g of olive oil into a bowl/mixer adding in the tested yeast.

  • Step 3

    Knead in the mixer adding up to about 250ml of water until a dough forms and knead using the window pain test.

  • Step 4

    Place into a bowl and proof for an hour.

  • Step 5

    Knock back, shape, then place in cooking container covered proofing for another hour before uncovering.

  • Step 6

    Add ~250ml of water to the ninja, select bread steam, and 170C for 25 mins.
