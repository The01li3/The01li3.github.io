Carrot Cake

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat oven to fan 160, and grease a 18cm loose bottomed round cake tin, line with baking paper.

  • Step 2

    Sift 230g self raising flour, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon, 1/2 tsp mixed spice and 1/2 tsp ground ginger into a bowl and mix.

  • Step 3

    Stir in 230g light brown muscovado sugar, 100g halved pecans and 260g grated carrots. Then add 115ml sunflower oil and 3 medium beaten eggs and mix.

  • Step 4

    Pour into cake tin and cook for 1 hour, or until a skewer comes out clean. leave in tin for 5 mins, then turn out and onto a cooling rack.
