Miso Soup

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    For a soft boiled egg 7-8 mins in boiling water, then place in cold water, 7 is for smaller eggs, 8 is for larger.

  • Step 2

    Soak 1 tbsp dried wakame in cold water for 5 mins.

  • Step 3

    Bring the 500ml dashi stock to the boil and add 3 tbsp miso paste, add tofu to heat through if using.

  • Step 4

    Divide spring onions and wakame between 2 bowls, and pour the miso stock overtop.
