Rice Noodle Salad

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Cook the 90g of rice noodle (cracked/cut to make short and easier to mix) and 100g of edamame as per instructions flash in cold water and leave to dry cool.

  • Step 2

    Cut 1/2 red pointy pepper into circles, and 1/2 red bell pepper,3 inches of cucumber and 1 carrot into batons (or using a julienne peeler) and place into serving bowl.

  • Step 3

    Put 1 tbsp peanut butter,1 tbsp lime juice,1 tsp lemon juice,1 tbsp light soy sauce,1 tsp dark soy sauce,1/4 tsp sesame oil,1 tbsp rice vinegar,1/2 tsp white sugar,1/2 tsp honey,1/4 tsp chilli flakes and 1 tsp sesame seeds into a shakeable container and shake to emulsify, adding water (maybe 1 tbsp) as needed.

  • Step 4

    Place noodles into serving bowl, drizzle with dressing mix and serve.
