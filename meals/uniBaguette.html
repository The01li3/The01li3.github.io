Instructions

  • Sauce

    Fry any fillings you wish, how you wish to cook them, then add in some flavour with some sauce.

  • Baguette

    Add the Baguettes to the oven to cook for 1 min less than the cook time, they should only be starting to brown.

  • Assembly

    Cut the baguettes in half "sandwich ways" add filling to th top, coat in cheese, capers... anything. then back into the over for 5 mins.
