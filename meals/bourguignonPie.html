Bourguignon Pie

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Notes:

    Since I make it in 2 sections for meat and vegetarian, this recipe will use half the mushrooms and half the meat, splitting everything else in 2 for 2 different pots with vegetarian in a frying pan, and meat in a cast iron.

  • Oven and Dish:

    Preheat oven to 180c, and prep a 20x28x5cm dish (we have been using one with a split in the middle for vegetarian and meat sides).

  • Step 1

    Add 3 chopped portobello mushrooms and 150g of quartered chestnut mushrooms to a frying pan and 250g of mince to a cast iron letting them brown slightly.

  • Step 2

    Mushrooms will cook first, add 1tbsp wine to the pan to deglaze (also add to mince to keep dishes even). Put mushrooms to one side along with any brown bits on the pan. Fry 3 large diced shallots and 3 sliced medium carrots. Add a little salt and pepper and cook for 6-7 mins on medium or until carrots are a little soft.

  • Step 3

    Add 3 minced garlic cloves, 1 tbsp minced fresh thyme leaves and 2 bay leaves cooking for 1 min before adding 2 tbsp tomato paste.

  • Step 4

    Add a splash of wine to deglaze again, mix thoroughly, add half of the mixture to the mince and add the mushrooms to the remains. Half of the remaining wine along with 125ml of vegetable stock in each pan and simmer for 25 mins-ish.

  • Step 5

    Stir in 1 tbsp of corn flour with a little water and 1/2 tbsp soy sauce in each pan, taste, season, remove and throw away bay leaves, then add the filling to the oven tray leaving to cool for 15 mins.

  • Step 6

    Roll out the sheet of puff pastry unless its an inch bigger than the pan, then place over the top cut 3 vent holes with a sharp knife, brush with milk and sprinkle with a little course salt.

  • Step 7

    Bake for 25 mins, or until browned, let sit for a few mins to allow sauce to thicken back up and serve.
