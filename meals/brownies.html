Instructions

  • Oven and Dish

    160c convention, 21cm square pan with a greaseproof paper sling.

  • Step 1

    Heat 113g of butter in a pan stirring (using a whisk is nice) to avoid burning butter solids, wait for it to brown a little. Then add 50g of cocoa powder, let it whisk and boil for a min.

  • Step 2

    Off heat and whisk in 1 can of sweetened condensed milk, after mixing whisk in 120g icing sugar (any small lumps are the butter solids), 2 tsp vanilla, 2 tsp molasses (ish, it doesn't measure well so just pour it in.) and a pinch of salt.

  • Step 3

    Add in 1 egg yolk (or whole egg + tsp baking powder if cakey version) and mix in well. Then mix in 90g of plain flour (120g for the cakey version). If adding chocolate chips etc set aside to cool for a bit and/or freeze the chips. Use the dog tongue spatula from here it gets thick!

  • Step 4

    Pour mixture into pan, it'll self level when cooking so don't worry about that. and place in oven at 160c ish for 30-40 mins. Knife test it for cook level.
