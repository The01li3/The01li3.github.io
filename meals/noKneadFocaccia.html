No Knead Focaccia

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Combine 500g bread/plain flour, 10g kosher/table salt and 4g instant dry yeast in a large bowl and whisk, add 400g warm water and stir with wooden spoon until no dry flour is left.

  • Step 2

    Add 20g/1 1/2 tbsp olive oil and stir, cover with plastic wrap and rest at room temp for 1 hour.

  • Step 3

    Using a lightly greased bowl scraper, fold dough over itself by lifting the edge and fold to the middle, turning 45 degrees each time, 6 times total. Re-cover and leave for 15 mins.

  • Step 4

    Repeat step 3 once more, then in the fridge for 18 hours, up to 3 days.

  • Step 5

    Remove dough and sit at room temp for 10 mins. Pour 28g/2 tbsp of olive oil into a 10 or 12 inch cast iron skillet and spread around, place dough into pan, turning in oil and gently spread.

  • Step 6

    Cover with plastic wrap and leave at room temp for 2 hours.

  • Step 7

    Oven at 200c (preheat a baking stone here if using). Stretch dough out to fill the pan, lifting the edges to let out any air. Dimple the surface with fingertips and cover with 20g/1 1/2 tbsp olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt.

  • Step 8

    Middle Shelf of the oven, rotating halfway through for 25-30 mins, let cool for 5 mins before moving to a cooling rack. Leave for 10 mins before slicing.
