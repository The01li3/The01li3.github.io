Currywurst

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Toast the spices: 4 tbsp of curry powder and 1 tbsp garam masala then place to one side with 1 tbsp of onion granules, 1 tbsp of garlic granules and 2 dry stock cubes

  • Step 2

    Add 150g of chopped onions, 4 minced garlic cloves, 2 dry stock cubes and 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar to a saucepan and fry.

  • Step 3

    Add 200g of chopped carrots, 2 tins of tomatoes and 400ml of water to te pan simmering for 40 mins.

  • Step 4

    Add in 2 tbsp of tomato puree and 2 tbsp of Worcestershire sauce then blend, adding salt and pepper as needed. Sauce can be cooked down a little if need be.

  • Step 5

    Cook required sausages as needed (veg,meat,bratwurst etc.). Then cut, plate and pour sauce over the top.
