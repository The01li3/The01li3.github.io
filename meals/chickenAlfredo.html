Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Add 250g chicken breasts to a pan, season with salt, pepper, 1/2 tsp dried oregano and 1/2 tsp dried basil, cook for 8-10 mins until fully cooked.

  • Step 2

    In the same pan melt 18g of butter, then add 4 minced garlic cloves cook until soft. Stir in the 22g of plain flour and combine, then slowly add 320ml of milk as needed.

  • Step 3

    Season with salt, pepper, 1/2 tsp dried oregano and 1/2 tsp dried basil (or all if not cooking chicken) and simmer until sauce thickens.

  • Step 4

    Add 20g parmesan stirring until melted. Add everything to a bowl with 5g parsley and 10g parmesan, mix and serve.
