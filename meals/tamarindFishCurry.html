Tamarind Fish Curry

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Blitz 6 garlic cloves, 1 red chilli, thumb sized ginger, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tbsp ground coriander and 3 tbsp of water.

  • Step 2

    Toast 2 tsp cumin seeds and 1 tsp fennel seeds, then add in the paste and fry for 3 mins.

  • Step 3

    Add 2 cans of chopped tomatoes and a can of water, add 200g trimmed halved green beans boil and simmer for 5 mins.

  • Step 4

    Stir in 1 tbsp tamarind paste, add 4 white fish fillets simmering for 10 mins. Take the lid off turn the fillets and simmer until fish is cooked and sauce is thick.
