Satay Sauce

Ingredients

Instructions

Serves 4

  • Step 1

    Add 120g peanut butter, 30g sambal, 100g ketjap manis, 2tsp sugar, 100ml water, 3.5g Indonesian spice mix into a saucepan and whisk.

  • Step 2

    Add 400ml coconut milk and bring to the boil for 2-3 mins.

  • Step 3

    After thickening add a little lime juice season with salt, add more water if needed, or keep reducing for a thicker sauce.
