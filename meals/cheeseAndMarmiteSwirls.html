Cheese and Marmite Swirls

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat oven to 200c fan and line a baking tray with greaseproof paper. Unroll the 320g ready rolled light puff pastry leaving it on its own greaseproof paper.

  • Step 2

    Add 1 tbsp marmite to a small bowl with 2 tsp boiling water and mix until smooth, spread this over the pastry leaving a 1cm gap on each of the short edges.

  • Step 3

    Sprinkle over the 40g reduced fat cheddar cheese and roll starting with a long edge, the gap will have filling squidged in. Use the paper its on to help, like sushi. Keep seem side down.

  • Step 4

    Using a large serrated knife cut into 16 place on pre-lined baking tray and cook in oven for 15-20 mins at 200c fan.
