Sushi Basics

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Wash 250g sushi rice until water runs clear, then soak in water for 15-30 mins before draining.

  • Step 2

    Add washed rice to 330ml cold water, bring to the boil, simmer on low for 10 mins covered, then 10 mins off the heat.

  • Step 3

    Mix 3 tbsp rice vinegar, 2 tbsp sugar and 1 tsp salt until dissolved. Add mix and rice to a bowl folding in the seasoning, cover and cool to room temp.

  • Step 4

    You can then roll with filling, nori on outside or in, coated in fried onions/sesame seeds, rolled or coned... you get the idea.
