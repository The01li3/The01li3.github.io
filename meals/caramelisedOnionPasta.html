Caramelised Onion Pasta

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Oven/Bake

    Pre heat to 180c

  • Step 1

    Place 300g of chunked white onions, 1 tbsp balsamic glaze with a little salt and pepper to an oven tray. Cover and bake for 1hr 15mins ish at 180 tossing every now and then.

  • Step 2

    Slice the tip of the bulb of garlic wrap in foil and cook for an hour-ish

  • Step 3

    Add 175g of shortcut pasta to boiling and salted water, cooking until al dente, keeping a cup of pasta water.

  • Step 4

    After cooking squeeze garlic into the onions and mix, add in 65g sun dried tomato, 75 mascarpone and 1 cup-ish pasta water.

  • Step 5

    Stir in the cooked pasta, 15g parmesan and a bunch of basil, season and serve.
