Chana Masala

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Fry the diced onion, 4 minced garlic gloves, 2 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp turmericand 1 tsp ground coriander for 5 mins.

  • Step 2

    Transfer to slow cooker if not already in it, and add 1 can of chopped tomatoes, 1 tbsp lemon juice and 2 cans of chickpeas stir well, and cook on low for 5 hours 30 mins.

  • Step 3

    Stir in 1 tbsp tomato puree and cook for 20-30 mins to thicken, season with sugar, salt, black pepper as needed (sugar will likely be needed to offset tomato acid).
