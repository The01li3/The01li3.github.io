Lentil Coconut Curry

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Blend 1 white onion, 1 garlic cloves, 1 chilli, 1 carrot and 10g ginger into a smooth paste and fry in saucepan for 4-5 mins.

  • Step 2

    Add 1 1/2 tbsp tikka masala paste, stirring to combine then add 1 can of green lentils, cooking for a couple mins until combined.

  • Step 3

    Pour in 220ml of coconut milk and 50-75ml of water and simmer for 10 mins adding peas for 5 mins, serve when thick.
