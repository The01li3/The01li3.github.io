Buffalo Cauliflower Casserole

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Mix 470ml vegetable stock, 118ml buffalo sauce, 22g nutritional yeast, 3 minced garlic gloves and 1 tsp onion powder in a jar, stir to dissolve stock cube if needed.

  • Step 2

    Pour 374g chickpeas into a casserole dish, top with 200g cauliflower then 182g uncooked brown rice, then the stock.

  • Step 3

    Cover and place in oven at 180c for 60 mins.

  • Step 4

    Remove and top with green onions, ranch, crispy onions etc.
