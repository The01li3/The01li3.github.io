Greek Style Baked Beans

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Fry 2 finely chopped onions for 10-15 mins until soft, add 2 minced garlic gloves and cook for a further 2 mins.

  • Step 2

    Add 2 cans of chopped tomatoes, 2 tbsp tomato puree, 2 tbsp fresh oregano leaves, salt and pepper bring to a simmer

  • Step 3

    If sharp add a little sugar/sweetener then add in the 2 cans of drained butter beans add ovenproof dish with a lid (casserole dish works well).

  • Step 4

    Oven for 30 mins at 160c. Remove lid, sprinkle 90g reduced fat feta cheese over the top, and cook for 5 more before serving.
