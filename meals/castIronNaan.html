Cast Iron Naan

Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Add the 113ml warm water, 1 tsp sugar and 10g active dry yeast to a jug, stir and leave in a warm place for the yeast to activate, it should get a little foamy.

  • Step 2

    Mix 281g plain flour, 125g yoghurt, 1/2 tsp salt and 1 tbsp oil, then add the yeast mixture.

  • Step 3

    Knead dough until the surface is shiny, about 10 mins by hand. cover and let rise in a warm place for an hour

  • Step 4

    Preheat the Cast iron pan, large hob, fraction over full down position (med... high-ish)

  • Step 5

    Split into 8 and roll into a 20cm circle using a rolling pin, leave on side, covered.

  • Step 6

    Lightly grease pan (oil on paper and wipe works), place dough down, wait till rise and flip, about 2-3 mins. Brush with melted butter.
